Inviting Members With an Invite Link
------------------------------------

If you're looking for a very quick way to invite members through different channels like iMessage and WhatsApp, send over an invite link.

Sending the Link
----------------

**Click "Copy Invite Link"**

![](/images/link1.png)

**Paste into Desired Channel**

![](/images/link2.png)

**View Member Activity Under "Customize & Insights" > "Insights" > "Engagement"**

![](/images/link3.png)

<Note>
**Note:** You can see which members signed up using the link you just sent out
</Note>
Recap
-----

You can copy the invite link, send it in your desired channel, and view your member activity.
