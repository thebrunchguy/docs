Changing the Join Link
-----------------------
The join link is what your members will see after sending them the HTML link. Let's customize this and make it yours.

Find the Join Link
------------------

**Under "Customize & Insights" head to "Club Settings" -> "General Settings"**

Recap

After navigating to "General Settings", you can change the end of the link to your custom name.
