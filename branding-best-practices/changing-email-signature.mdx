Changing Your Email Signature 
---------------------

Personalizing your email signature is a simple yet effective way to convey professionalism and brand identity in your communications.

Change the Email Copy
---------------------

**Under "Customize & Insights" head to "Club Settings" > "Communications"**

![](/images/email-sig1.png)

Recap
-----

You can directly change your email copy under the "Communications" section. Customizing your email signature ensures that every email sent from your platform carries a consistent and professional touch.
