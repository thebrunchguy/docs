Welcome to the Insights page, where you can view past intros you’ve made and monitor member engagement and feedback. 

---

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Intros Made">
    In the **Intros Made** tab, you can view matches between members and a brief overview of information, including status, duration, and rating.

    ![](/images/Insights-Intros-Made.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Engagement">
    In the **Engagement** tab, you can view events, their dates and times, the members involved, and the intro’s details. You can also sort by Intro Round, event, and member via the filters at the top of the page.

    ![](/images/Insights-Engagement.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Feedback">
    In the **Feedback** tab, you can choose a specific Intros Round or member to view their feedback.

    ![](/images/Insights-Feedback.png)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
