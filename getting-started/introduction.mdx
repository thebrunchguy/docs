### **Start Here**

Welcome to Intros AI, the platform designed to enhance your community by facilitating high-quality connections among members.

With Intros AI, you can customize how members are matched within your community, automate introductions, track insights, and much more!

This guide will help you get started with Intros AI. If you’re new to setting up an Intros Club or creating an account, you’re in the right place!


### **Let's dive in!**

Let’s start with creating an account: [head here](https://docs.intros.ai/setting-up-slack/creating-an-account) for a walkthrough of creating an account and starting your first Intros Club.

Now, let’s better understand [how to navigate the Dashboard](https://docs.intros.ai/getting-started/dashboard). Here, you can view all onboarding, membership, and introduction progress.

Finally, let’s learn how to invite new members to our Intros Club. There are a variety of ways to do this, including:
- Inviting via CSV File
- Inviting via Onboarding Link
- Inviting via Email

This [Interactive Demo](https://intros-ai.navattic.com/ls40mrn) will walk you through the process of inviting new members, and if you still have questions, check out this [step-by-step process](https://docs.intros.ai/getting-started/members).

Finally, let’s kick off scheduling introductions between your members. There are a variety of ways to schedule introductions, which you can learn about more in-depth through these step-by-step instructions or this Interactive Demo.

If you get stuck along the way,continue checking out our docs, ping us in the chat (lower right), or email supprt@intros.ai.
