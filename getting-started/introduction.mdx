Welcome to Intros AI, the platform for building stronger connections in your community through facilitating high quality introductions between members.

This is your guide to getting started with Intros AI. If you’re new to setting up an Intros Club or account with us, you’re in the right place!

### **What is Intros AI?**

Intros AI is a platform aimed at making high quality, personalized introductions between members of your community so they can stay engaged and find their people. 

Within Intros AI, you can customize your algorithm for how members of your community should be matched, automate and monitor introductions between them, track insights like match fit, and much more.
