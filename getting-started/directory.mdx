Welcome to the Directory page! As the admin, you can view the overall member experience, where they can explore and request introductions, see their upcoming scheduled matches and activities, as well as update their preferences and profile settings.

![](/images/Directory-Toggle-Off.png)

<Note>
You can click the toggle icon on the right hand side of the page and select "No" to not have a Directory if you don't want your members to request intros on their own.
</Note>

---
### Member Directory Experience
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Members">
   Under **Members**, your members can directly request intros with other members, as well as use filters e.g. location, company, etc. at the top of the section to find customized matches.

![](/images/Directory.png)

</Tab>
  <Tab title="Upcoming">
  Under **Upcoming**, your members will see all of their upcoming matches that are scheduled within this section.

![](/images/Directory-Upcoming.png)

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Activity">
  Under **Activity**, your members will be able to see all their activity and can filter by: all intros made, who proposed times, scheduled and completed matches, as well as leave feedback on each intro.

![](/images/Directory-Activity.png)

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Preferences">
  Under **Preferences**, your members can edit their answers to the onboarding questions to get the best Intros experience.

![](/images/Directory-Preferences.png)

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

### Member Profile Experience
When your members login through the magic link (passwordless), they are initially greeted with the below, "Your Profile." Here, members can upload their headshot, update their email, pronouns, phone number, bio, etc.

![](/images/Directory-Profile-Info.png)

As they scroll down, members can also update information on their career and education, such as their job title, company, university, majors or concentrations, location, LinkedIn, etc.

![](/images/Directory-Career.png)

Finally, your members also can connect their calendar for easier scheduling, set their time zone, enable automatic bookings, as well as set their available hours for calls. They also can choose to leave the community if they so choose as well (below scheduling).

![](/images/Directory-Scheduling.png)
