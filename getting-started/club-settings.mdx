Welcome to the Club Settings page, where you can view General Settings, Branding, Team, Matching, and Communications. 

---
View our interactive demo below!


<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Settings">
    In the General Settings tab, you can edit your Intros Club Name, Intros Club Join Link, how to handle new members, change preferred          timezone, and select whether or not to host a directory.

    ![](/images/general-settings-1.png)

    > If you select “Yes” for hosting a directory, members will be able to view other members’ profiles and manually request Intros.
    > 
    Customizing the join link and club name are a strong way to make your Intros Club stand out.

![](/images/changing-join-link-1.png)

 > Insert Your Text After "/join/".
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Branding">
    You can edit and upload your Intros Club logo, change the primary and secondary colors for Email Buttons, and change the Join Form           Background. 

    ![](/images/branding-1.png)

**Changing Your Logo**

    If you haven't updated your logo yet, or if you're looking to refresh your branding, you can easily change your logo by uploading a JPG or PNG file.

![](/images/changing-your-logo-1-optim.png)

<Note>
**Note:** When you change your logo, Intros AI will apply the update to every communication option. So, this will appear in your welcome emails, invitations, etc.
</Note>

**Customizing Button Colors**

Customizing your email button colors is a great way to make your Intros invitations stand out in an inbox.

![](/images/button-colors-1.png)

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team">

    Expanding your team is a great way to distribute administrative tasks and enhance your community management.

![](/images/adding-team-members-1-optim.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Matching">
    You can customize your Intros matching experience, such as the question weighting for your personalized matching algorithm.

    ![](/images/matching-1.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Communications">
    You can fully customize your club’s communication, including things like email signature, name displayed on emails, and the ability of       members to reply to you.
    
    Finding and customizing your display name and email signature helps members identify you. 

    ![](/images/communications-1.png)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
