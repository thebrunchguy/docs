Welcome to the Club Settings page, where you can view General Settings, Branding, Team, Matching, and Communications. 

---

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Settings">
    In the General Settings tab, you can edit your Intros Club Name, Intros Club Join Link, how to handle new members, change preferred          timezone, and select whether or not to host a directory.

    ![](/images/club-settings-2-optim.png)

    > If you select “Yes” for hosting a directory, members will be able to view other members’ profiles and manually request Intros.
    > 
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Branding">
    You can edit and upload your Intros Club logo, change the primary and secondary colors for Email Buttons, and change the Join Form           Background. 

    ![](/images/club-settings-3-optim.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team">
    You can invite admins for your community here. 

    ![](/images/club-settings-4-optim.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Matching">
    You can customize your Intros matching experience, such as the question weighting for your personalized matching algorithm.

    ![](/images/club-settings-5-optim.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Communications">
    You can fully customize your club’s communication, including things like email signature, name displayed on emails, and the ability of       members to reply to you. 

    ![](/images/club-settings-6-optim.png)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
