Welcome to the Club Settings page, where you can view General Settings, Branding, Team, Matching, and Communications. 

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Settings">
    In the **General Settings** section, you can edit your Intros Club Name, Intros Club Join Link, how to handle new members that are added via Onboarding Form or Zapier, and change your preferred timezone.

     <Tip>
    **Pro Tip:** Customizing the Join Link (insert your text after "/join/") and Club Name is a strong way to make your Intros Club stand out.
</Tip>

    ![](/images/Club-Settings-General.png)
    
   
  
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Branding">
    You can edit and upload your Intros Club logo, change the primary and secondary colors for Email Buttons, and change the Join Form           Background. 

    ![](/images/Club-Settings-Branding.png)

**Changing Your Logo**

    If you haven't updated your logo yet, or if you're looking to refresh your branding, you can easily change your logo by uploading a JPG or PNG file.

![](/images/changing-your-logo-1-optim.png)

<Note>
**Note:** When you change your logo, Intros AI will apply the update to every communication option. So, this will appear in your welcome emails, invitations, etc.
</Note>

**Customizing Button Colors**

Customizing your email button colors is a great way to make your Intros invitations stand out in an inbox.

![](/images/button-colors-1.png)
