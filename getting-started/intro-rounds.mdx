Welcome to the Intro Rounds page, where you can see your upcoming and past Intros and schedule Intro Rounds.

---
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Viewing Intro Rounds">
    Within the Intro Rounds page, you can see your upcoming Intros with members you’re matched with. You also view records of past Intros. 

    ![](/images/intro-rounds-2-vFF.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Scheduling Intro Rounds">
    You can select the date and time to send the invitation, schedule the introductions, choose how your members can schedule, and even          customize the calendar invitations.

    You can select to repeat these rounds every week, bi-weekly, or monthly and whether you want to send opt-in or opt-out invites. 

    > **Opt-in**: You’ll get to ask if the member would like to join the club.
    > 

    > **Opt-out**: You have the option to leave or not join the Intro.

    ![](/images/intro-rounds-3.png)

    ![](/images/intro-rounds-4.png)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
